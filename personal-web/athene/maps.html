A toponímia das cantigas

Place names in the songs

Study of toponyms according to the genre in which they are mentioned. The Galician-Portuguese cantigas can be divided into three basic genres: male-voiced love poetry, called cantigas de amor; female-voiced love poetry, called cantigas de amigo; and poetry of insult and mockery called cantigas d'escarnho e de mal dizer. Most of the songs defined as minor genres are tenções, that is cantigas that take the form of a debate.
